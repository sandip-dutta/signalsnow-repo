Jake Og Flat

This board has been inspired by our monthly web show,  ”Every Third Thursday”. We come up with some pretty creative ideas for this show and —while we haven’t been able to mount a paintball gun in every board— we ‘ve incorporated some of the other technologies into our line.

Choosing Flat Technology—or no camber—is an excellent choice for park riding. The lack of camber in your board allows you to engage the entire edge. Flat boards also give the rider better leverage for many tricks because you’re never working against the board.

Signal cores are lightweight. They are milled in our own factory with Poplar wood and  built by alternating grain structure and combined with high quality resin. These cores are strong and retain their pop.

Every board that Signal builds is hand made in our southern California factory. We’re very proud of this. Your support increases our strength in promoting US manufacturing, US jobs and quality craftsmanship.

These boards are a favorite year after year because they have a soft flex, are durable and can handle anything you get yourself into. From camber to flat, wide waist to narrow, there is a PARK board here for everyone. Inspired from ETT, this year’s graphic has cassette tape under the topsheet —like the Boombox episode!

  • Built for today’s terrain parks and city streets— Built to take a beating
  • Magnum ABS sidewalls
  • Sintered bases on all models
  • “Park Profile” core, which is thinner on the nose and tail but has a standard thickness between your feet
  • The alternating grain structure of the Poplar core provides maximum strength and maximum pop
  • Lightweight triaxial glass on the top and bottom of the core gives the Park a soft but more controlled flex
  • Nylon 8210 top material provides a softer torsional flex
  • Carbon stringers from tip to tail for added pop and strength
